<objective>
Replace the Twilio Conference transfer approach with Vapi's native warm transfer. Delete all Twilio Conference infrastructure (4 files), add the transferCall tool to the Vapi assistant config, update stall instructions to tell the AI to call transferCall, and simplify the webhook handler to work with warm transfer events instead of the orchestrateTransfer module.

Purpose: The Twilio Conference approach required maintaining a separate Twilio phone number, conference webhooks, a transfer orchestrator state machine, and complex call-leg management. Vapi's warm-transfer-experimental mode handles all of this natively -- hold audio, user dial-out, transfer assistant, call merge, AI exit. This architectural pivot dramatically simplifies the codebase.

Output: Clean codebase with Vapi warm transfer configured end-to-end. No Twilio Conference code remaining.
</objective>
